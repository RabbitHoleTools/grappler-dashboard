────────────────────────────────────────
  📱 ON YOUR PHONE (iPhone / Android)
────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Open the file in your browser

  • Go to your Downloads or Files folder
  • Tap "grappler-dashboard.html"
  • Choose "Open in Safari" (iPhone) or "Open in Chrome" (Android)
  
  ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Open in your browser, NOT in the Files app.
     Files app preview doesn't run the dashboard properly.

STEP 2: Install it like an app

  ON iPHONE:
  1. Tap the Share button ⎙ (the square with arrow) at the bottom
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" (top right)
  4. ✅ The dashboard icon appears on your home screen

  ON ANDROID:
  1. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in Chrome
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add"
  4. ✅ The dashboard icon appears on your home screen

That's it. Now it opens full-screen like a real app. 
All your data saves to your phone automatically.

────────────────────────────────────────
  💻 ON YOUR COMPUTER
────────────────────────────────────────

Just double-click "grappler-dashboard.html" and it opens in your browser.
Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

────────────────────────────────────────
  ✨ HOW TO USE IT
────────────────────────────────────────

📊 DASHBOARD TAB — See your stats
  Total sessions, submissions, mat time, weekly chart.
  Tap any session to delete it.

✏️ LOG TAB — Log a training session
  Tap the session type, duration, intensity, enter your subs.
  Takes about 10 seconds. Data saves automatically.

📚 TECHNIQUES TAB — Build your technique library
  Add techniques with category and difficulty.
  Mark which ones are "Part of My Game."

⚙️ MORE TAB — Goals, Weight, Backups
  Set training goals. Track weight and wellness.
  Use ⬇ Export to back up. Use ⬆ Import to restore.

────────────────────────────────────────
  💾 ABOUT YOUR DATA
────────────────────────────────────────

All your data is saved on YOUR device — not in the cloud.
• Close the page? Data stays.
• Clear browser history? Data stays (it's in local storage).
• Get a new phone? Use ⬇ Export to save a backup file,
  transfer it to your new phone, and tap ⬆ Import.

To back up: Tap the ⬇ button (top right) → saves a .json file
To restore: Tap the ⬆ button (top right) → pick your backup file
