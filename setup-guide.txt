    4. ✅ The dashboard icon appears on your phone

  📱 ON ANDROID:
    1. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in Chrome
    2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
    3. Tap "Add"
    4. ✅ The dashboard icon appears on your phone

  💻 ON COMPUTER:
    Just bookmark the link. Works on any browser.

────────────────────────────────────────
  ✨ HOW TO USE IT
────────────────────────────────────────

📊 DASHBOARD TAB — See your stats
  Total sessions, submissions, mat time, weekly chart.

✏️ LOG TAB — Log a training session
  Tap session type, duration, intensity, enter your subs.
  Data saves automatically to your phone.

📚 TECHNIQUES TAB — Build your technique library
  Add techniques with category and difficulty.

⚙️ MORE TAB — Goals, Weight, Backups
  Use ⬇ Export to back up. Use ⬆ Import to restore.

────────────────────────────────────────
  💾 ABOUT YOUR DATA
────────────────────────────────────────

All data stays on YOUR device — not in the cloud.
• Close the page? Data stays.

  transfer it to your new phone, and tap ⬆ Import.
